Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a club?
You can join any club by attending their meetings and speaking with the club coordinator. Most clubs have a sign-up sheet at their first meeting of the semester. You can also reach out to club leaders via email.
How do I book a lab or study room?
Lab and study room bookings can be made through the campus portal. You need to log in with your student credentials, select the resource you want to book, check availability, and reserve your slot. Same-day bookings may be available at the resource's front desk.
Are there any fees for joining clubs?
Most clubs are free to join, but some specialized clubs (like robotics or photography) may have a nominal membership fee to cover materials and equipment usage. Any fees will be clearly communicated during the first meeting.
How can I propose a new event?
Students can propose new events by submitting an event proposal form available at the Student Affairs office. The proposal should include event details, expected attendance, required resources, and a faculty sponsor. The event committee reviews proposals monthly.
